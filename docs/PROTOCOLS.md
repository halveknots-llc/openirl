# Protocols

OpenIRL treats SRT and SRTLA as primary IRL contribution paths, RTMP/RTMPS as compatibility paths, RIST as a pro contribution option, and WHIP/WHEP as browser/WebRTC preview and future contribution paths.

## Recommended defaults

- Phone or backpack contribution: SRT or SRTLA.
- Local routing: MediaMTX.
- OBS source: local SRT or RTMP URL.
- Platform output: OBS-native output to Twitch, Kick, YouTube, or a custom endpoint.
- Producer preview: WHEP where available.
